John Carpenter

Filmmaker, screenwriter and musician

John Howard Carpenter(born January 16, 1948) is an American filmmaker, screenwriter, musician, and composer. Although Carpenter has worked with various film genres, he is associated most commonly with horror, action and science fiction films of the 1970s and 1980s.

Most Notable Films

  • Assault on Precinct 13 (1976)
  • Halloween (1978)
  • The Fog (1980)
  • Escape From New York (1981)
  • The Thing (1982)
  • Big Trouble in Little China (1986)
  • They Live (1988)
  • Christine (1983)
  • Starman (1984)

Television

  • Body Bags
